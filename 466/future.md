title: Future Solutions
---

Continuing to develop regulations and laws for AI usage is an important step in creating lasting solutions. AI companies need to work with medical professionals in order to recognize signs and symptoms, analyze data, and provide valuable insight to potential mental health concerns. THe most helpful solution is for AI to work in tandem with human therapists but not everyone has access to this. It is all about systems that know their limitations and can be held accountable and prioritizes safety.

More robust solutions could include more rigorous testing in clinical settings and developing AI models with full-bodied datasets specifically catered towards mental health. Current AI models are trained off of general datasets that may pose risk to algorithm bias. This could cause insufficient assistance to certain demographics. AI testing in clinical settings would ensure that it can provide safe and reliable assistance to patients. As well as follow guidelines to improve outcomes.
